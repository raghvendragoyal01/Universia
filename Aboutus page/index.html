Welcome to Universia—a thriving community of space enthusiasts united by a shared passion for the cosmos. At Universia, we aim to inspire curiosity and promote the wonders of astronomy across the nation. We specialize in bringing the universe closer to you through: High-quality Telescopes designed for all levels of stargazers Stargazing Sessions under breathtaking night skies Astronomical Workshops to explore the science and beauty of the cosmos Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or just starting your journey, Universia offers a gateway to the stars, creating unforgettable experiences that spark a lifelong love for space exploration. Join us, and let’s discover the universe together!

In this journey, all you need to bring along is curiosity. Starscapes will be your uplink to the stars, your platform to the skies. Your gateway to the Universe.
